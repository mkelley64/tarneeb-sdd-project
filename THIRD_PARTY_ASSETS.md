# Third-Party Assets

## xCards card faces

The 52 card-face PNG assets under `Tarneeb/Assets.xcassets/card_face_*.imageset`
come from the `png/face` directory of Xadeck/xCards:

https://github.com/Xadeck/xCards/tree/master/png/face

Only the standard 52-card deck face images are included. Joker images from that
source are not included.
